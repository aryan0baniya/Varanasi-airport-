Lal Bahadur Shastri International Airport - Varanasi

About the New Terminal

The new terminal at Lal Bahadur Shastri International Airport, Varanasi, is designed to handle over 5 million passengers annually. It is equipped with modern facilities such as advanced security systems, spacious lounges, and duty-free shopping areas. The terminal highlights Varanasi’s rich cultural heritage through its architectural style, combining modernity with tradition.

About Varanasi Airport

The development of Lal Bahadur Shastri International Airport, Varanasi, has been approved with a budget of ₹2,869.65 crore. This expansion will increase the airport's annual passenger capacity from 3.9 million to 9.9 million. The new terminal building will cover 75,000 square meters and handle 6 million passengers. Additionally, the runway will be extended to 4,075 meters, with parking for 20 aircraft. The project focuses on making the airport eco-friendly and sustainable.
